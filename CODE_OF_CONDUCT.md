# Code Of Conduct

Reckonry follows a concise Contributor Covenant style code of conduct.

## Our Standards

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, data, and evidence.
- Respect privacy and confidentiality.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, and abusive behavior.
- Assume financial and tax topics require care, precision, and humility.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, intimidation, discriminatory language, doxxing, sharing private financial data, sustained disruption, or knowingly misrepresenting financial or tax behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior violates this code of conduct.

Reports can be sent privately to the maintainers. A public maintainer email is not currently exposed; do not include sensitive reports in public issues.
